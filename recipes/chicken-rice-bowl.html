Chicken Rice Bowl

Description

A simple chicken rice bowl that consists of tender seasoned chicken on a bed of rice, accompanied by crisp vegetables for a balanced and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast sliced into bite size pieces
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Minced Ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rice
  • Mixed vegetables

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rinse rice, then cook with water until done. Fluff and set aside.
  3. Cook marinated chicken in a heated pan until cooked through and browned. Set aside.
  4. Stir-fry the mixed vegetables in olive oil.
  5. Assemble the bowl with the chicken, cooked rice, and mixed veggies.
