Steak

Description

A juicy and flavorful steak that is very straightforward.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless steak
  • Salt and pepper
  • butter
  • Vegetable oil
  • rosemary
  • garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Prepare the steak by letting it sit outside the refrigerator and reaching room temp to cook even.
  2. Season both sides of the steak generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Place a cast iron pan over medium-high heat and add oil.
  4. Place the steak on the hot pan and allow it to sear for about 4 minutes for medium-rare. Flip the steak and cook for similar time.
  5. About a minute before the steak is cooked add in butter, garlic cloves, and rosemerry for extra flavor. Tilt the pan and spoon the melted butter onto the steak.
  6. Remove the steak from the pan let it rest on a cutting board or plate for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute resulting into a jucier steak.
  7. Slice the steak against the grain to ensure tenderness. Plate the steak and serve.
